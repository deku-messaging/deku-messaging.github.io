How it works

About DEKU

Deku is an end-to-end encrypted messaging and photo sharing app. With deku users can send and receive text messages and share images through sms. Provides users with the functionality to forward their SMS messages to their online servers.

End-to-End encryption

No third party can read your messages due to its encryption

Offline

You don't need to be online to send Pictures to your love ones. You can do that With just text messages.
